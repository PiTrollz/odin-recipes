PinkShaker Recipe

Description

This recipe is made of just 6 ingredients and is very easy to do.

You just need a blender and a shaker.

Ingredients

  • 200ml Milk
  • 100g Greek Yogurt
  • 60g Instant Oats
  • 50g Whey protein (flavour of your choice)
  • 1 Banana
  • 50g Red Fruits

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients mentioned above into the blender
  2. Mix everything until you see a vibrant pink color
  3. Pour the shake into the shaker
